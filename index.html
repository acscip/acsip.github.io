Our Purpose

Building bridges between
Chad and Pune

ACSIP connects, supports, and represents Chadian students studying in Pune. We promote unity, cultural exchange, and academic excellence — ensuring no student faces this journey alone.

Learn About Us

Academic Support

Guiding students through admissions, transfers, and university life.

Cultural Pride

Celebrating Chadian heritage through festivals, food, and fashion.

Community Care

Housing help, FRRO guidance, and a supportive family away from home.

What We Do

Our Activities

Group Trips

Exploring India together — building unforgettable memories and lifelong friendships across cities and landmarks.

Independence Day

Annual celebration in Delhi with ambassadors, where association leaders from across India unite to discuss student affairs.

Regular Meetings

Monthly gatherings to discuss plans, resolve member concerns, and strengthen our association's direction.

University Events

Football tournaments, food festivals, and fashion shows — representing Chad with pride at top Pune universities.

Collaborations

Joining events hosted by other international student associations to foster cross-cultural community bonds.

FRRO & Legal Help

Assisting students with mandatory FRRO registration, residency permits, and all Indian legal formalities.
