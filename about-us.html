Who We Are

A family away from home

The Chadian Student Association in Pune exists to make life easier for every Chadian student who chooses this city for their academic journey. Whether you're arriving directly from Chad or transferring from another Indian state, our mission is to support you — practically, emotionally, and academically — so you never feel alone in this transition.

For students coming to Pune for the first time, we offer full guidance through the admission process. We help you apply to universities, colleges, or English language courses, explain each step clearly, and stay with you until your admission is confirmed.

Once your admission is sorted, we assist with the legal formalities required for international students — including FRRO registration, which is mandatory to ensure your residency permit is valid under Indian law. We help you prepare documents, book your appointment, and follow up on approvals.

Our Mission

What drives us every day

ACSIP aims to create a strong community among Chadian students in Pune by promoting academic success, cultural exchange, and mutual support. We organize social, cultural, and academic events to bring everyone together — and to remind every member that home is not that far away.
