Our Activities

Celebrating culture, sport, and community — all year long.

ACSIP Activities
Annual

Independence Day

We celebrate Chad's Independence Day every year in Delhi, with ambassadors in attendance. The presidents and vice presidents of each Chadian association across India fly to Delhi to oversee the event. The purpose is to discuss the issues facing Chadian students in India, and the embassy looks for collective solutions.

Regular

Group Trips

We organise fun trips around Pune and India. It's the perfect way to take a break, get to know each other, and create unforgettable memories. To make this possible, we all contribute a small amount to cover the cost of the trip — making it accessible and fun for everyone.

Friendly Matches

Internal friendly football matches organised for members to stay active, have fun, and strengthen team spirit within the association.

University Events
🏆 Won

Football Tournament

Some universities organise football competitions every year — country competitions where every nation with students in Pune participates. We represented Chad in Pune University and Symbiosis University competitions. Our team is led by the sports coordinator and the captain, who selects the players. We played with pride and had a great time.

Food Festival

Universities organise food festivals where every country presents its cuisine. International students come to taste diverse foods, and chefs judge and award the best food and decorations. We participated at Pune University, Symbiosis University and MIT University — a great opportunity to showcase Chad's rich gastronomy.

Flag Presentation

We proudly display the Chadian flag at every event organised by universities — representing our nation with dignity and pride.

Fashion Show

We showcase our traditions and traditional attire in university fashion shows, bringing the beauty of Chadian culture to the stage.

Collaborations

Collaborative Football

We actively participate in every football event organised by other international student associations in Pune — building friendships beyond borders.

Cultural Events

We join cultural events hosted by other associations in Pune — sharing our heritage and learning about other cultures in a spirit of unity and respect.
